Cancellation Policy and Subscription Management

We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise, and you may need to modify or cancel your subscription. Please review our cancellation policy below:

Cancellation:

We kindly request a minimum of 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule a cleaning appointment.

Cancellations or rescheduling requests made less than 24 hours in advance may incur a cancellation fee.

The cancellation fee will be equal to 20% of the booked services or $40, whichever is more.

Cancellation fees will be charged to the credit card provided during the appointment booking process.

Subscription Management:

You can manage your subscription online through our website.

Update your payment information, schedule modifications, or cancel your subscription at any time through your online account.

For any assistance with subscription management, please contact our customer support team.

We strive to provide flexibility and convenience to our customers while ensuring the smooth operation of our cleaning services. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our cancellation policy or subscription management, feel free to reach out to us.
