History

Established in 2018

Began more than 25 years ago in the kitchen of a home bakery, washing the dishes after everything was baked and receiving a grandmother, lessons in gardening at the age of seven. Volunteering cleaning, organizing, and inventory intake services to churches, before venturing out to create this business as suggested by Mr. Oliver and Ms. Francis. Cleaning for many people who lived on a fixed income for many years, now adding gardening and landscaping. Every home, office, garden, and landscaping we provide has an impact on lives both directly and indirectly, what once was a chore and an interest turned into a love. The EEb Cleaning Services NY Inc Team alters environments, minds, and lives today. Most clients are genuinely delighted and report feeling relieved of a burden, a load, or another obligation. The encounter is wonderful.
